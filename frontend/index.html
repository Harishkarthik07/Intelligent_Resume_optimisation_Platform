How it works

From upload to optimized in 3 steps

No manual editing. No guessing. Just paste your resume and the job description — we handle the rest.

01
Upload your resume
Upload your existing resume in PDF, DOCX, or TXT format. We extract and analyse the full text automatically.
02
Paste the job description
Copy any job description and paste it in. Our ATS engine extracts required skills and scores your resume against it.
03
Get your optimized PDF
Gemini AI rewrites your resume to match the role, fills missing keywords, and generates a professionally formatted PDF.
Features

Everything you need to get shortlisted

Built for software engineers, ML practitioners, and tech professionals targeting competitive roles.

ATS Scoring Engine
Keyword match (60%) + semantic similarity (40%) composite score. 200+ skills across languages, frameworks, cloud, and ML.
